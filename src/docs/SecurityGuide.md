# Ethio Herd Connect Security Guide

## የደህንነት መመሪያ | Security Guide | Qajeelfama Nageenya

![Security Shield](../assets/icons/security-shield.svg)

## Overview | ምልከታ | Ibsa Waliigala

This guide explains how Ethio Herd Connect keeps your information safe, even with poor internet connection. The app works offline and protects your data.

## Key Security Features | ዋና ዋና የደህንነት ባህሪያት | Amaloota Nageenya Ijoo

### 🔒 Works Without Internet | ያለ ኢንተርኔት ይሰራል | Interneetii Malee Ni Hojjeta

![Offline Mode](../assets/icons/offline-mode.svg)

- Sign in even when offline
- Your data is saved on your phone
- Syncs automatically when internet returns
- Clear indicators show when you're online or offline

### 🔑 Secure Login | ደህንነቱ የተጠበቀ መግቢያ | Seensa Nagaa

![Secure Login](../assets/icons/secure-login.svg)

- Password protection keeps your account safe
- Visual password strength indicators
- Fingerprint login option (if your phone supports it)
- Automatic logout after inactivity

### 🔄 Account Recovery | የመለያ መመለሻ | Deebisuu Herrega

![Account Recovery](../assets/icons/account-recovery.svg)

- Easy steps to recover your account if you forget your password
- Recovery using phone number
- Visual verification code input
- Step-by-step instructions with pictures

### 🛡️ Data Protection | የመረጃ ጥበቃ | Eegumsa Ragaa

![Data Protection](../assets/icons/data-protection.svg)

- Your livestock and farm data is encrypted
- Only you can see your sensitive information
- Control what information is shared
- Request deletion of your data at any time

### 📱 Shared Device Security | የጋራ መሳሪያ ደህንነት | Nageenya Meeshaa Waliinii

![Shared Device](../assets/icons/shared-device.svg)

- Safe to use on shared phones
- Quick logout option
- Automatic session timeout
- Option to clear all data from the device

## Privacy Controls | የግላዊነት ቁጥጥሮች | To'annoo Iccitii

![Privacy Controls](../assets/icons/privacy-controls.svg)

You control your data:

1. Choose what information to share
2. Decide how long to keep your data
3. Control who can see your livestock information
4. Request complete deletion of your account

## Security Tips | የደህንነት ምክሮች | Gorsa Nageenya

![Security Tips](../assets/icons/security-tips.svg)

1. **Strong Password** | **ጠንካራ የይለፍ ቃል** | **Jecha Darbii Cimaa**
   - Use a password you can remember but others cannot guess
   - Mix letters, numbers, and symbols if possible
   - Don't use your name or phone number as password

2. **Logout When Finished** | **ሲጨርሱ ይውጡ** | **Yeroo Xumurtan Ba'i**
   - Always logout when letting someone else use your phone
   - Tap the logout button in the menu

3. **Keep App Updated** | **መተግበሪያውን ያዘምኑ** | **App Haaromsi**
   - Install updates when available
   - Updates improve security

4. **Report Problems** | **ችግሮችን ያሳውቁ** | **Rakkoolee Gabaasi**
   - Tell us about any security concerns
   - Use the "Help" section in the app
