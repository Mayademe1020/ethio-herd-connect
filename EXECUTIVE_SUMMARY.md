# Ethiopian Calendar Integration - Executive Summary

**Project:** Ethiopian Calendar Integration  
**Status:** ✅ COMPLETE - Ready for Deployment  
**Date:** January 20, 2025

---

## 🎯 **WHAT WE BUILT**

A complete calendar preference system that allows users to view all dates throughout the application in either the **Ethiopian Calendar** or **Gregorian Calendar**.

### **Key Feature:**
Users select their preferred calendar once in their Profile, and **every date** in the entire application automatically displays in their chosen calendar.
